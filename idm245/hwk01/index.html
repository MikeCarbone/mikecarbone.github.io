3D Free Kick

I like playing this game because I'm actualy able to improve! I started out missing shots but as I learned the mechanics I was able to get higher and higher scores. Small details like the goal shaking when you score made goals very rewarding. Something that can be improved is instructions on how to make the ball go airborne. I did it once by accident, but its mostly unclear.

Parking Passion

Link

I like this game because it was addicting. Who would've thought parking cars would be so fun? I really liked just driving this car around the little world. It made me remember how complicated video games =/= fun. Only thing that could be improved would be the inclusion of a mini-map or some sort of overview of the map. Kept crashing because I didn't know where to go.

Pizza Realife Cooking

Link

This game was pretty fun, I really liked cutting up the toppings. The pace of play was really slow and was annoying how I had to click something before cutting it. If I had to change something, I'd have them go from start to finish with the pizza process, including the dough and sauce part. The graphics were nice, though.
